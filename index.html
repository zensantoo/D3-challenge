Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

Select from the various headings of Poverty, Age, Household Income, Obesity, Smokers, and Without Healthcare. Each selection changes the State data in circles on the graph. The small circles can then be selected to view the values for that state.

Texas, at the top of the graph, having a poverty rate of 17.2 % with 24.9 % without healthcare and Massechusetts having a poverty rate of 11.6 % but 4% rate without healthcare.

Majority of those without healthcare are in the 35 - 45 age range. But you will also find, that selection of obesity and smokers doesn't shift the average age range by much at all! The average age of all this data from 2014 seems to fall within that range.
